Privacy Policy for FocusFalcon (Chrome Extension)

Last updated: February 2026

FocusFalcon is a productivity browser extension designed to help users stay focused by blocking distracting websites. Your privacy is important, and this extension is built with a privacy-first approach.

Information Collection

FocusFalcon does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data. All settings and usage data are stored locally on your device using Chrome’s extension storage.

The extension may store the following locally:

  • Blocked website domains
  • Time-based blocking schedules
  • Focus session timers
  • Selected Focus Guardian preferences

Data Usage

All stored data is used solely to provide the extension’s core functionality. No data is sent to external servers or third parties.

Permissions

FocusFalcon requests only the permissions necessary to block websites and manage user settings. These permissions are used strictly for the extension’s intended purpose.

Third-Party Services

FocusFalcon does not use analytics tools, advertising networks, or third-party tracking services.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this privacy policy, you may contact the developer via the Chrome Web Store listing.
