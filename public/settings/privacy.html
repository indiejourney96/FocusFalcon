Privacy Policy for FocusFalcon (Firefox Extension)

Last updated: February 2026

FocusFalcon is a browser extension that helps users maintain focus by blocking distracting websites. The extension is designed to operate entirely on the user’s device.

Data Collection

FocusFalcon does not collect any personal data. No information is transmitted outside of the user’s browser.

The extension stores the following information locally:

  • User-defined blocked websites
  • Time-based blocking rules
  • Focus session status and timers
  • Selected Focus Guardian settings

Data Sharing

FocusFalcon does not share data with the developer or any third parties. No analytics, tracking, or advertising services are used.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally using Firefox’s extension storage APIs and remains under the user’s control. Removing the extension deletes all stored data.

User Control

Users can modify or delete their settings at any time through the extension’s settings page or by uninstalling the extension.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated to reflect future changes. Updates will be posted on this page.

Contact

For questions regarding this privacy policy, please contact the developer via the Firefox Add-ons listing.
